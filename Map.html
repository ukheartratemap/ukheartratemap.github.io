UK Heart Rate Map

This is the UK Heart Rate Map. The map will be updated as more data is collected. The map displays data on resting heart rate in the UK for different demographic groups. To view data for one age range or sex, uncheck the boxes for the demographic groups above it.

This map makes use of UK Biobank data, which can be subject to selection bias.
